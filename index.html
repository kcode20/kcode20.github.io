Khristian Brooks is a student of the City College of New York majoring in Computer Science. She has experience in creating apps for the iOS platform. She also had the opportunity to create her own unique mobile game entitled Clustered. During the academy, she got to meet Alexis Ohanian, co-founder of Reddit. She did an interview with him on FOX Buisness News. (Click here to see the video!)

She aspires to create her own non-profit organization that will provide web and mobile services for other non-profit organization, to help raise awareness for the many issues being addressed worldwide. She also asprires to create a Bible Game entitled 66-Letters. In her free time, she is authoring a book named "She is Like Me, I am Like Her".
